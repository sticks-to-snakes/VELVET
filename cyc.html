CYC
Description
VELVET Cyc lights deliver high-quality, color-accurate cyclorama illumination with tunable white, green/magenta correction and smooth theatrical dimming to zero at any setting. A combination of precision optical lenses, independent batten control, and adjustable beam angles creates an exceptionally even vertical light spread across the cyclorama. The unique articulated system also saves valuable stage space by allowing the fixtures to be positioned just five feet from the cyclorama while maintaining uniform coverage Their silent, fan-free design and recycled low-carbon aluminum housing ensure durability, sustainability, and a 50,000-hour LED lifespan. Full local and wireless control—including DMX-RDM, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi Art-Net, and the GOYA app—combined with industry-leading efficiency makes the Cyc series a powerful, low-energy studio solution.
