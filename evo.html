EVO
Description
VELVET EVO delivers ultra-soft, natural light and vivid color from its 5LED engine, wrapped in a lightweight, low-carbon aluminum body that is silent, durable, and IP54 weather-rated for any location. It offers total local and wireless control—including DMX, Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, and the GOYA app—along with fast RDM addressing and advanced color tools. Industry-leading efficiency allows full-power operation on AC or standard batteries, with no output loss and a 50,000-hour LED lifespan.
