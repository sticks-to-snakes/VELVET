KOSMOS 400
Description
VELVET KOSMOS is a compact, high-output color-tunable Fresnel with a V5LED engine, flicker-free dimming to 12,000 fps, adjustable white light from 2500–10,000K, and motorized zoom from 13°–56° for remote beam control. Its true borosilicate Fresnel lens delivers classic, consistent shaping and beautiful wraparound light, while touchscreen, DMX-RDM, wireless protocols, and the GOYA app provide complete creative control. Silent multi-mode cooling, a lightweight recycled-aluminum body, 50,000-hour LED life, and industry-leading efficiency—even on batteries—make KOSMOS a powerful, sustainable studio and location tool.
