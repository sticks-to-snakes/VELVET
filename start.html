Description
The VELVET Start panel features a lightweight, shock-resistant recycled aluminum body with silent fan less cooling for dependable on-set operation. On-board and DMX RDM control with Master-Slave linking simplify studio workflows, while the high-efficiency built-in PSU delivers exceptional light output with minimal power draw. An IP41 rating, advanced thermal design, and 50,000-hour LED lifespan ensure long-term reliability and performance.
