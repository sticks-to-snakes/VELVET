Description
The perfect combination of high-intensity output and dense diffuser material creates a broad pool of light ideal for studios with high ceilings. Delivering an astonishing 3,700 fc when bounced into Hi-Lite diffuser material (skirt and target), the VELVET Spacelite produces powerful, soft illumination that descends evenly to cover large areas. Its exceptional efficiency—balancing power draw and output—makes it the ultimate lantern for tall stages, AND the entire fixture can be installed by a single operator.
