Description
The VELVET Articulated luminaires let you choose between one and six bars, with three width options—30, 60, or 120 cm—to match your output needs. You can customize the housing color, select precise beam angles using the filter and lens system, and expand creative control with mechanical angle adjustment. Optional DMX and power in/out connections add flexibility for seamless integration into any setup.
